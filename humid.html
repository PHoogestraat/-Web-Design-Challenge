Analaysis

A plot of latitude vs. humidity was obtained from the data set (figure B-1). The data was divided into northern (figure B-2) and southern hemispheres (figure B-3) to obtain linear regression values. Plots for the northern hemisphere had a r^2 value of 0.0451 indicating no correlation between latitude and humidity. The positive slope indicates humidity increases with increasing latitude which would not be consistent with the trend. Southern hemisphere had linear regression value of 0.1395 r^2 demonstrating a very week correlation or no correlation. The slope of the line is negative which would be consistent with the trend of humidity decreasing in cooler climates.

This data does not decisively demonstrate a correlation between an increase of humidity to an decrease of latitude.
