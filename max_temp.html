Max Temperature

Analaysis

Temperature to latitude data was as expected. Temperatures near the equator trend higher than other regions (Graph: Temperatures in world Cities based on Latitude). A second graph was constructed to overlay data from both hemispheres to contrast trends (Graph: Temperature in World Cities base on Latitude). Linear regression analysis shows a similar comparison between temperatures for both hemispheres. the slopes for both Northern hemisphere and Southern hemisphere were similar (North -0.83: South -0.67). R2 values correlation imply a medium correlation to the data plotted (North 0.68: South 0.46).
